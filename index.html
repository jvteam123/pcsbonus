Guided Setup

Welcome to the PCS Bonus Calculator! This guide will walk you through the basic steps to get started.

You can drop your combined `csv` file or `shp` and `dbf` files in the Data & Projects panel to upload your data.

Once your project is loaded, you can customize your Bonus Multiplier and Payout Type. The default is 'Per Task' with a multiplier of 1.

Ready to calculate? Click the "Calculate" button below the settings.

The results are now displayed in the "Results" section. You can see a quick summary of each tech's points and estimated payout.

To see the full details, go to the Leaderboard or TL Summary panels using the menu on the left.

That's it! You are ready to start calculating.

For more advanced features, check out Manage Teams and Advanced Settings in the main menu.
