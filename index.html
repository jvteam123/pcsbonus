Important Information

This tool is a proof-of-concept for the new PCS Bonus System. It is designed to assist with personal bonus estimation. The features and calculations are based on information and formulas provided internally.

Key Features:

  • Supports parsing of standard PCS project files (.dbf and .csv).
  • Automatic bonus calculation based on quality tiers.
  • User-configurable bonus tiers and calculation values.
  • Team management for filtering and summaries.

Disclaimers:

This is an estimated calculation for informational purposes only. It is not a guarantee of actual compensation and may differ from official salary offers or personal expectations.
